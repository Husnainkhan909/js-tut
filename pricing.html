Pricing for all shapes & sizes

The benefits of the Critter Credits model are many.

The approach allows us to offer competitive pricing to every size business, without limiting the available features in the
app. We won,t nickel and dime you while adding part-time employees and we don,t pass fees on to your clients. We give
you control over your software expense, whether you,re a new business just getting started, a stable business with an
established roster of clients, or an actively scaling business with grand ambitions. Pay less up front for a lower month to br month expense, or buy in bulk for reduced total spend, as credits never expire. The options are endless.

Find a detailed explanation of our pricing structure and a pricing calculator at pricing.critter.pet.
